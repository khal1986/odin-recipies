lemon-pancake

beautiful soft pancakes

if theses pancakes were any lighter they would float off the plate

and i did not need to separate the eggs and whip the whites.

ingredients

  • 3/4 cup cold water or milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter

steps

  1. whisk water and baking soda together in a mixing bowl add ricotta cheese lemon,

  2. zest,vegetable oil, sugar, egg, and vanilla. whisk till smooth.

  3. add melted butter, lemon juice, and 1 cup plus to spoons of flour.
