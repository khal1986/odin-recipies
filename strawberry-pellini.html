strawberry-pellini

a nice drink for mothers day

a perfect cocktail that tastes smooth to the tongue and you can drink all day

ingredients

  • 3 cups strawberries,hulled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup confectioners sugar
  • 1 tablespoon brandy
  • 1 1/4 cup chilled wine
  • 3 large strawberries

steps

  1. blend 3 cups strawberries, confectioners sugar, and brandy together

  2. divide the strawberry mixture among 3 champagne flutes

    3. top each with 1/2 cup sparkling wine.
